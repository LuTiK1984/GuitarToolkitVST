# Code of Conduct

GuitarToolkit is a small music-tooling project. The goal is to keep discussion
useful, respectful, and welcoming for musicians, developers, and DAW users.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and patient with other users and contributors.
- Assume good intent, especially when someone is reporting an audio, DAW, or
  setup problem that may be hard to reproduce.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Use clear issue titles and include reproduction details when reporting bugs.
- Respect that maintainers may prioritize stability and product polish over
  feature volume.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic comments that make project work harder.
- Demanding unpaid work or urgent support as an entitlement.

## Reporting problems

If a conversation becomes harmful or unproductive, open an issue or contact the
maintainer through the repository owner profile. Include links and context so the
maintainer can review the situation.

The maintainer may hide comments, lock threads, close issues, or block users
when needed to keep the project healthy.
